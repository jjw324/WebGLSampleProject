Controls

  • Flying Camera
    • Up and down arrows to move forward and backward
    • Left and right arrows to move left and right
    • Z/z to roll counter-clockwise/clockwise
    • X/x to pitch upward/downward
    • C/c to yaw leftward/rightward
  • Circling Camera
    • Up and down arrows to increase/decrease altitude
    • Left and right arrows to increase/decrease radius
  • t to toggle camera mode
  • r to reset the camera
  • b to generate a bunny
  • k to remove a bunny
